Welcome to
BK Speech Therapy

Dr. Bhupendra Kumar Mishra, a highly qualified Audiologist & Speech Pathologist with over 13 years of experience, is renowned for his expertise in speech therapy, audiology, and cochlear implant rehabilitation.

Holding qualifications such as BASLP, M.Sc, DHLS, and CDA(UK).

He is currently serving as a Consultant at SIPS Super Specialty Hospital, Lucknow.

As a Visiting Consultant (Cochlear Implant) at Holy Family Hospital, New Delhi.

He is also the Principal of Beautiful Minds, Lucknow.

His Credentials

Dr. Mishra specializes in early detection and intervention for speech, hearing, and autism-related issues, auditory-verbal therapy for cochlear implant children, and cleft care management. His skills include auditory comprehension therapy, oral motor placement therapy, and the use of advanced tools like DR.SPEECH and VAGHMI software for articulation and voice therapy. He is also proficient in multidisciplinary approaches for neuro-developmental disabilities, feeding and swallowing therapy, and parent/caregiver counseling.
